Gurujibiharwale

 

AT Gurujibiharwale, WE ARE PERSISTENT TO DELIVER TIMELY SOLUTIONS AND RESOLUTIONS IN THE LEAST POSSIBLE TIME.

 

We at Gurujibiharwale have had a long journey. From being a tiny mobile repair store in the crowded market place in the locality, we have moved on to become a sleek, swanky and an end-to-end mobile solutions provider with a strong offline and online presence. With the mobile phone market teeming with mobile phone models and technologies, and with new facets being launched each day.

Gurujibiharwale has been a proud recipient of many honors. It was selected in the Top 400 social impact startups under the smart fifty startups competition conducted by the Dept. of Science and Technology. Repair my mobile has received a seed fund under the INVENT scheme sponsored by the Dept. of Science and technology, Government of India and is currently incubated by Indian Institute of Management, Calcutta Innovation Park (IIMCIP).

Gurujibiharwale has helped train thousands of rural youth to become micro-entrepreneurs. Gurujibiharwale finished in the Top 20 in the Tata Social Enterprise Challenge(T-SEC), 2019. Gurujibiharwale is proudly partnering with Social Alpha- A TATA trust– to create many micro-entrepreneurs in rural India by skilling them with mobile repair know-how and providing logistical and technical support to setup mobile repair shops.
